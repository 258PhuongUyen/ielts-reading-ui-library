🔬 Red Light in the Deep Sea

Đoạn văn Từ vựng & nghĩa
A. Nico Michiels is an ecologist from the University of Tübingen in Germany who spends part of each year in Egypt, where he dives in the Red Sea, observing fish life and gathering data on its coral reefs.
ecologist : nhà sinh thái học
observe : quan sát
gather data : thu thập dữ liệu
coral reef : rạn san hô
In September 2007 he decided to find out how far red light could penetrate the ocean depths.
penetrate : xuyên qua
depth : độ sâu
Seawater absorbs different colours at different depths, and as an experienced diver, Michiels was aware that red light is extinguished not far below the surface whereas blue-green light penetrates deeper.
absorb : hấp thụ
extinguish : biến mất
surface : bề mặt
whereas : trong khi
To find out the depth at which red disappeared, he attached a special plastic filter to his dive mask.
attach : gắn
filter : bộ lọc
In theory, once he reached about 15 metres, he should have been plunged into darkness.
in theory : về lý thuyết
plunge into : rơi vào
darkness : bóng tối
Instead, something totally unexpected happened.
unexpected : bất ngờ
Sure enough, 20 metres down it was as dark as night.
sure enough : đúng như dự đoán
With no light from the surface, they were effectively black and had become invisible.
effectively : thực chất
invisible : không nhìn thấy
But then I saw a group of goby fish with bright red eyes lit up against the background.
goby : cá bống
background : nền
B. Even with the red filter removed, Michiels could pick out them without much trouble once his eyes grew accustomed to the gloom.
remove : loại bỏ
pick out : nhận ra
accustomed to : quen với
gloom : bóng tối mờ
It seems strange that no diver or researcher had spotted all this red before.
researcher : nhà nghiên cứu
spot : phát hiện
On that one dive Michiels discovered three fish species with prominent red markings.
species : loài
prominent : nổi bật
marking : dấu hiệu
C. But how can fish appear red where there's no red light?
appear : xuất hiện
Ordinary red pigments look red because they reflect red light while absorbing other wavelengths.
pigment : sắc tố
reflect : phản xạ
wavelength : bước sóng
He suspected fluorescence.
suspect : nghi ngờ
fluorescence : huỳnh quang
Fluorescent pigments receive light of one wavelength and emit light of a longer wavelength.
emit : phát ra
The most likely explanation was that blue and green light triggered fluorescent red in fish.
explanation : giải thích
trigger : kích hoạt
D. With only a week left, Michiels photographed as many fish as possible.
photograph : chụp ảnh
Back in Germany, he installed them in his lab.
install : lắp đặt
lab : phòng thí nghiệm
He confirmed that the fish did fluoresce.
confirm : xác nhận
The fluorescence could be traced to specialised pigment cells beneath the scales.
trace : truy ra
specialised : chuyên biệt
scale : vảy cá
These cells contain guanine crystals which scatter light.
contain : chứa
crystal : tinh thể
scatter : phân tán
It's probably a fluorescent protein built into the crystals.
protein : protein
built into : gắn vào
E. Michiels began a systematic search for red fluorescence in reef fish.
systematic : có hệ thống
search : tìm kiếm
They identified about 50 species with red fluorescence.
identify : xác định
The markings tend to be on the body, especially near the head and eyes.
tend to : có xu hướng
distribution : sự phân bố
This suggests red fluorescence has a specific function: communication.
function : chức năng
communication : giao tiếp
Animals use fluorescence to boost visibility of body parts used to signal.
boost : tăng cường
visibility : khả năng nhìn thấy
signal : tín hiệu
The fluorescence gives fish a colour that otherwise wouldn't exist.
otherwise : nếu không thì
F. Red light doesn't travel far through water, suggesting signals are private.
travel : truyền đi
private : riêng tư
There are several lines of evidence to support this.
evidence : bằng chứng
support : ủng hộ
Closely related species have different markings, suggesting a role in recognition.
closely related : liên quan gần
recognition : nhận diện
Michiels suspects red fluorescence helps fish blend in.
blend in : ngụy trang
Corals glow a faint red too.
glow : phát sáng
faint : mờ
A fish that glows red would be hard to distinguish.
distinguish : phân biệt
The scorpionfish lies still until food swims past.
lie still : nằm yên
prey : mồi
If red plays a role, fish must be able to see it.
play a role : đóng vai trò
Fish are assumed to have eyes tuned to blue-green light.
assume : cho rằng
tuned to : thích nghi với
Most marine fish are thought incapable of seeing red.
marine : biển
incapable : không có khả năng
One exception is the seahorse.
exception : ngoại lệ
As for other fish, it remains to be seen.
remain to be seen : còn phải xem
